OUR PROGRESS

Louis 'LYRO' Dupont

21
04

Our incredible team is still really focused on the project, knowing that we have to present a new version of the project next Monday. But we wanted to share with you all the amazing stuff we worked on those past weeks. This post will be a perfect occasion for you to learn more about our team and who is working on Vroom Boom. I hope you will enjoy it !

Thibaut 'ThibThib' Mattenet - Project Leader

Hi Thibthib, could you present yourself a little bit ?

Hello guys ! I am Thibthib, 20 years old, and a little bit immature *laugh*

What are you doing in the development of Vroom Boom ?

I am responsible of the level design and team management. I really want our players to enjoy playing on the game, and I truly think that playing on beautiful maps is a very important element for players. I also need to study the gameplay, so that our maps are perfectly adjusted to our gameplay, and players can do amazing stuff on those maps.

Okay ! And what is your main inspiration when you create your maps ?

Mmm... That is a really good question ! I think I am mainly influenced by Mario Kart, because it is one of my favourite games, especially the Mario Kart : Double Dash, on GameCube. This one was absolutely amazing ! I think all the ballons or sun fight maps are really appropriate to our kind of gameplay, so yeah, I guess this is my main inspiration.

What do you expect of Vroom Boom ?

I know it might seems a little bit exagerated, but I would love to see the game on Steam Greenlight, and of course greenlighted by the community. And my secret fantasy is to see Vroom Boom rewared Game of the Year, like my favourite game ever, Rocket League (After Vroom Boom of course).

Perfect ! Let's hope it will happen ! A last word for all your fans ?

Keep supporting us, and we hope we will do a great job and that we won't disapoint you. We are always listening to you and we want your feedbacks and your ideas !


Alexandre 'Tofuzinzin' Girard - Art Director / Sound Designer

Hello Tofuzinzin, told us more about you ?

I am Alexandre, aka Tofuzinzin, I play a lot of video games, and I love the idea of creating my own game, it is very exciting !

So, what is your role in Explosive Rainbow Poop ?

I am the lead artist of the team, reponsible of the cars design. I created almost all the cars in our game, using pre-existing assets from games like Jak or Mad Max.

Oh ! As a lead artist, what are your inspirations ?

I love the Mad Max universe. I played a lot the Mad Max game to catch the atmosphere, and to try to retranscript it in our game. I really love this post apocalyptic ambiance. When we talked about the context around our game, we decided to place our game in this post apo area. But we decided not only to show sand maps, with empty landscapes. But we wanted to show various places, such as Aztec montains, destroyed cities, or even unexpected maps, such as small cars maps. We just hope you will enjoy this artistic direction !

What do you prefer in the game

I love the SandShark. It's a long love story : for me, the SandShark is a car from Jak 3, and back in my childhood, I played a lot Jak 3 and Jak X, and those games are still today my main inspirations. This car is really beautiful, I love its design.

One last question : what are your expectations about Vroom Boom ?

I hope Vroom Boom will be a fun game, with a gameplay and many features that will attract players. I also hope of course that we will get a good grade with it.

A last word for your fans ?

There is no sin but ignorance - All is dust

Heu... Right...

I want to thank Tofuzinzin and Thibthib for their time.

See you soon !

VROOM BOOM 0.2 (Alpha) IS HERE !

Louis 'LYRO' Dupont

01
03

We officialy release the first public build of Vroom Boom, in its 0.2 version. This release is opened to everyone who wants to play Vroom Boom. What you just need to do is to clic on the download button on the main page !
We would really love to have your feedbacks, and to have a list of all bugs that you met. If you want to send a report, please contact us at contact@vroom-boom.com.

Features :

  • Menu : you can select a multiplayer game to join (only Aztec)
  • Garage : you can't launch the garage for now. (Beta version)
  • Settings : you can previsualize our settings system, even if settings are not currently saved.
  • Map : we finished Aztec for this Alpha release, to show player what kind of maps we are working on.
  • Multiplayer : you can try the different controls and movement of the car. But please notice that life is not implemented for now. Gamemodes are not implemented neither.

We wanted to show you our progress throught those first mounth of work. We hope that it show all of our motivation to work on this project.
Please notice that we will surely release some transitional versions of Vroom Boom before the next oral.

See you soon !

- Explosive Rainbow Poop Team
